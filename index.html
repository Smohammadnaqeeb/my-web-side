Hello everyone, My name is Shaik Mohammad Naqeeb, and I’m currently a student pursuing my Bachelor of Engineering, in my 2nd semester, at Shridevi Institute of Technology. It’s been an exciting journey so far, filled with learning new concepts, adapting to new environments, and building a strong foundation for my future career in the field of technology. From a young age, I’ve been fascinated by how things work—especially in the world of computers and the internet. That curiosity led me to explore web design, which has now grown into one of my favorite hobbies. I really enjoy creating new web page designs, experimenting with layouts, colors, and elements to make a site not just functional, but also visually appealing and user-friendly. Every time I build something from scratch, I feel a sense of accomplishment, and it motivates me to keep improving my skills. Apart from that, I also love reading books. Reading gives me a chance to learn new things, discover different perspectives, and sometimes just escape into other worlds. Whether it’s books related to technology, self-growth, or even fiction, I find value in every page. Reading not only helps me gain knowledge, but it also improves my focus and creativity, which is something I carry into my other interests too—especially design. What I enjoy most about web development and design is how creative and dynamic the field is. Technology is always changing, and there’s always something new to learn—whether it’s a new framework, a design trend, or a tool that can make work more efficient. I look forward to becoming a professional in this space someday, where I can combine both technical skills and creativity to build something meaningful and impactful. At this stage of my life, I’m focused on building a strong foundation—learning as much as I can, practicing consistently, and pushing myself out of my comfort zone. I believe that success comes from constant effort and the willingness to learn from failures. I’m not afraid to make mistakes, because each one is a stepping stone to improvement. In the future, I see myself working in a field where I can design and develop web applications or software solutions that solve real problems. I also hope to keep growing as a person—developing good communication skills, building strong relationships, and being someone who adds value wherever I go. To sum up, I’m a curious and driven person who loves books, enjoys web design, and is passionate about learning. I’m excited about the future, and I believe that with hard work and dedication, I can achieve great things.
